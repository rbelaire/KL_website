Company email on your phone

Set Up Email — iPhone

Add your @geauxacs.com mail to Apple Mail so new-lead alerts reach your phone. Tap the button below on your iPhone:

⬇  Download Email Profile On iPhone, this must open in Safari
  1. 1Tap Download Email Profile above. Tap Allow when it asks to download a configuration profile.
  2. 2Open Settings — you'll see Profile Downloaded near the top.
  3. 3Tap it, then tap Install (top right). Enter your phone passcode if asked.
  4. 4Enter your @geauxacs.com email password when prompted, then finish.
  5. 5If a "Cannot Verify Server Identity" message appears, tap Continue — that's normal and still secure. Done!
Must be Safari — If this page opened inside another app (like a text or Gmail), tap the share icon and choose Open in Safari first. iPhone only installs the profile from Safari. Don't have your email password? Reset it anytime in your Hostinger webmail.

⚠ If something seems off

Not seeing new leads?

  1. Check your email spam / junk folder and mark those messages "Not spam."
  2. Send yourself a test through the form on the site.

Website not loading?

  1. Try another device or network first — this rules out your own connection.
  2. Confirm the domain hasn't hit its renewal date at Hostinger.
